Save One Save All Initiative

If truly the peace of a nation begins in the home of its people, then the peace of this universe can be achieved if we who occupy it determine to toe the path of harmony with ourselves and our environment. We are all inmates of the same planet and have no cause to distort the peace we inherited here. Our much coveted universe is threatened hence, the time has come for us to be more wary of our activities which have long been adversely impacting our globe than ever. Let us make our world a safe and peaceful home.If one is unsafe, all is unsafe. Save one person or thing now and always in order to save all.
