What We Do

Education

We concert our efforts towards availing ourselves of the opportunity to make a difference in the society by sensitizing people on the essence of education.We provide educational aids and subvention without bias for schools where and when needed prior to government intervention, monitor academic perfomance of students in less-priveleged communities, encourage them and intervene in order to bring about the best in them, and consequently bring them close to being at par with the ones in the urban areas.We bridge the gap between the people with little or no access to education and the ones who have. Even though we do not discriminate educational support at higher level from others, of greater focus and concern to us is education for children both in the rural and urban areas alike most especially in Africa.We also create an avenue for innovation exchange in order to ease sharing of creative, evolving and advancing education practices for the tutors and students alike at all levels.

Humanitarian Relief

We focus on giving succour to the victims of man-made and natural disasters ranging from (but not limited to) war, earthquakes, floods, tsunamis, drought, and famine by providing food, shelter, cooking supplies, medical care, and other forms of support at our beck and call for the traumatized and vulnerable.Man-made and natural disasters deprive and strip the affected people of the sanity, peace and tranquility required for growth and day to day activities furtherance if left unaided.At Save One Save All,we believe fanatically in harmony between us humans and our environment and will never fold our arms watching the birth of any occurrence that is at odds with this belief in global peace, or its growth if this cancerous event perhaps find its way into our environment without expeditious intervention.

Climate Change

Our choice name speaks so loud that we are as deeply concerned about the safety of ONE as well as the safety of ALL.Human age-long mundane, business and industrial activities have been great threat to the only planet that has agreed to shelter us.We have overtly and covertly, advertently and inadvertently been eroding this safe universe of ours.Both our terrestrial, celestial and aquatic activities have adulterated, threatened as well as endangered our natural resources.We sensitize on and advocate for the need for protection and sustainable development of natural resources and their ecosystems in order to prevent threat to their survival, and consequently make the world a better place for all to live.We advocate for policy-making and change of policy where and when need arises for the benefit of all.The universe is crying for our attention and change of attitude and Save One Save All has made itself available as a change agent.
